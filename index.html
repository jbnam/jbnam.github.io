JB's $L$-function database

Let \(\chi\) be a primitive Dirichlet character of conductor \(f_{\chi}\) and order an odd prime \(k\). Moreover, let $f$ be a primitive form of weight $2$ for $\Gamma_0(N)$. Define $$ L(f, s, \chi) = \sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{\chi(n)a_n}{n^s}. $$
