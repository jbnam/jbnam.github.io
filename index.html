Research Works in Number Thoery

My name is Jungbae Nam. This site is dedicated to my research works for computational number theory including the collections of the central and algebraic values of (Hasse-Weil) $L$-functions $L(E, s, \chi)$ of elliptic curves twisted by families of primitive Dirichlet characters of some fixed orders that I have been computing since the COVID-19 pandemic.

Database in this site contains two collections of data:

  1. The central values $L(E, 1, \chi)$ and some values associated with characters $\chi$
  2. The algebraic values of those central $L$-values including their algebraic parts and norms, which were used for [1].

For more detailed information about the database, visit Database.

If you have a comment/question regarding this database, please feel free to contact me at x.y@concordia.ca or x.y@gmail.com where x and y are my first and last name respectively.
