Jungbae Nam

I am a number theorist. I obtained my Ph.D degree in Mathematics & Statistics, Concordia University, Montréal, Québec, Canada in 2019 under the supervision of Hershy Kisilevsky and Chantal David. After that I worked as a Research Assistant professor in Mathematics & Statistics, Concordia University, Montréal, Québec, Canada until May 2020.

My research areas are Arithmetic and statistics of the special L-values of modular forms; Computational algebraic/analytic number theory; Post-quantum cryptography. I enjoy the programmings focused on the high performance number theoretic computations.
